Introduction

At Fast ZED Soroban, we respect user privacy and are committed to protecting personal data.

Information We Collect

  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • Account type (Trainer / Trainee)
  • Trainee's date of birth
  • Exercise results (up to 15 sheets per trainee)
  • Device ID / User ID
  • Crash logs, diagnostics, and analytics
  • Temporary data for live calculations or TTS (not stored)

Why We Collect User Data

  • App Functionality: To execute app features such as authenticating users and activating functionality.
  • Analytics: To analyze app performance, usage patterns, detect and fix bugs or crashes, and improve future performance.
  • Account Management: To configure or manage user accounts, allowing creation, login, and verification.

  • No advertising, tracking, or profiling of children.
